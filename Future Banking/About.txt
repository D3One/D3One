FutureBanking.ru is a platform dedicated to innovations in the banking sector, including digital solutions, fintech and process automation.

The site publishes analytical materials, cases of successful implementations and forecasts for the development of banking services. Particular attention is paid to digital transformation, payment security and customer experience.

Some topics discussed on the resource: open banking, blockchain, artificial intelligence in finance, marketing strategies and regulatory changes.

The site was created by the company "Reglament" and is designed to allow users to follow the trends and opportunities of the future in the banking business.

Website address: futurebanking.ru.
